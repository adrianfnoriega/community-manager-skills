# Noriega Group — Business Context

> This is the single source of truth for brand voice, rules, personas, and content strategy.
> All CM skills load this file before executing. Update here — not in individual skills.

## Company

- **Name**: Noriega Group
- **Industry**: Luxury real estate development
- **Location**: Punta Cana, Dominican Republic
- **Website**: noriegagroup.com

## Brand Voice & Tone

- Aspirational, professional, warm. Luxury without being cold.
- Educate on investment with authority but in accessible language.
- Mix Spanish and English strategically by channel and audience.

### Tone by Channel
- **Instagram / TikTok**: Visual, emotional, punchy
- **LinkedIn**: Investment-focused, data-driven, market perspective
- **Facebook**: Storytelling, longer context, community feel
- **Email**: Professional, educational, aspirational — like a knowledgeable friend who's a real estate expert
- **DM responses**: Professional, warm, helpful. Never salesy — be genuinely useful.

## Visual Identity

- **Style**: Luxury, clean, modern
- **Investment content**: Gold/dark tones
- **Lifestyle content**: Bright Caribbean colors
- **Brand kit**: Always use the Canva brand kit for consistency
- **Emojis** (sparingly, max 3-4 per post): 🏙️ 🌴 🏖️ 💎 📈 🔑 🇩🇴 ✨ 🏗️ 💼

## Hashtags

- **Core brand** (always include 1): #NoriegaGroup #PuntaCanaRealEstate
- **Secondary**: #InversionInmobiliaria #LuxuryLiving #RepublicaDominicana #CONFOTUR

## Content Pillars

1. **Education** — CONFOTUR, buying process, ROI, DR market
2. **Project** — Construction updates, amenities, renders, available units
3. **Lifestyle** — Punta Cana, gastronomy, beach, golf, Caribbean luxury
4. **Testimonials** — Client stories, investor success, social proof
5. **Team & Culture** — Who we are, values, expertise

## Buyer Personas

| Persona | Age | Focus | Language |
|---------|-----|-------|----------|
| **Inversionista latino** | 35-55 | ROI, CONFOTUR tax savings, rental yield, market comparisons | Spanish |
| **Lifestyle buyer** | 40-60, anglophone | Second home/retirement, amenities, Caribbean lifestyle, community | English |
| **Diaspora dominicana** | Varies, US/Europe-based | Reconnect with RD, family legacy, pride of ownership | Spanish/Spanglish |

## Key Selling Points

- **CONFOTUR**: Dominican law exempting buyers from taxes for up to 15 years (ITBIS, transfer tax, rental income tax). Top investor selling point.
- Premium Punta Cana location
- Luxury amenities and architectural design
- Strong ROI and rental income potential

## Rules (Non-Negotiable)

- **NEVER** reveal exact prices — always direct to schedule a call or request personalized info
- **ALWAYS** include a clear CTA on every post, story, and email
- Max 3 hashtags visible in caption; rest in first comment
- English posts must be labeled for anglophone audience
- Nothing goes live without explicit user approval
- Spanish is the default language
